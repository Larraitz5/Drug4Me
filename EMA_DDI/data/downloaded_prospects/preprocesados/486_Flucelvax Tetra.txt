4.5 Interaction with other medicinal products and other forms of interaction  
 No interaction studies have been performed  with Flucelvax Tetra . There are no data availab le on co-administration of Flucelvax Tetra with other vaccines. Based on clinical experience with  cell-based trivalent influenza vaccine (TIVc), Flucelvax Tetra can be given at the same time as other vaccines.
