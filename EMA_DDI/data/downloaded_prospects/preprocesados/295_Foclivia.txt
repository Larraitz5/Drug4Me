4.5 Interaction with other medicinal products and other forms of interaction 
 Foclivia may be co -administered with non-adjuvanted seasonal influenza vaccines, and immunisation should be carried out on separate limbs.  
 There are no data on co -administration of Foclivia with vac cines other than non -adjuvanted seasonal influenza vaccines.  If co-administration with another vaccine is considered , immunisation should be carried out on separate limbs. It should be noted that the adverse reactions may be intensified.
