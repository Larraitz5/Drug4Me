4.5 Interaction with other medicinal products and other forms of interaction 
 The safety, immunogenicity and efficacy of co-administration of Zabdeno with other vaccines have not been evaluated, and therefore, co-administration is not recommended.If Zabdeno must be given at the same time as another injectable vaccine(s), then the vaccine(s) should always be administered at different injection sites. Do not mix Zabdeno with any other vaccine in the same syringe or vial.
