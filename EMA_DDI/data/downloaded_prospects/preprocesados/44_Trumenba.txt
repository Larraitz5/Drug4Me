4.5 Interaction with other medicinal products and other forms of interaction  
 Trumenba  can be given concomitantly with any of the following vaccines: Tetanus Toxoid, Reduced Diphtheria Toxoid, Acellular Pertussis , and Inactivated Poliovirus Vaccine (Tda 
 P -IPV), Quadrivalent Human Papillomavirus vaccine (HPV4), Meningococcal Serogroups A, C, W, Y conjugate vaccine (Men 
 ACWY) and  Tetanus Toxoid, Reduced Diphtheria Toxoid , and Acellular Pertussis Vaccine Adsorbed (Tdap).   
 When given concomitantly with other vaccines Trumenba must be administered at a separate injection  site. 
 Trumenba should  not be mixed with other vaccines in the same syringe.
