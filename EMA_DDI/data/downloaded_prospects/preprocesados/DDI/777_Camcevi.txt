> Since androgen deprivation treatment may prolong the QT interval , the concomitant use of leuprorelin with medicinal products known to prolong the QT interval or medicinal products able to induce Torsade de pointes such as class  IA (e.g. QUINIDINE, DISOPYRAMIDE) or class  III (e.g. AMIODARONE, SOTALOL, DOFETILIDE, ibutil ide) antiarrhythmic medicinal products, METHADONE, MOXIFLOXACIN, ANTIPSYCHOTICS, etc. should be carefully evaluated (see section  4.4).
