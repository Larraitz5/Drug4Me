> Concurrent and/or sequential use of inhaled liposomal AMIKACIN is not recommended with other medicinal products with neurotoxic, nephrotoxic or ototoxic potential that can enhance aminoglycoside toxicity (e.g. diuretic compounds such as ETHACRYNIC ACID, f urosemide  or intravenous MANNITOL) (see section  4.4).
