 The validity of the test  result may be affec ted if the patient is currently bei ng treated with antibiotics or a proton-pump inhibitor or has completed a cours e of treatment with these drugs.  The results may be affected in general by all treatment s interfering with H.pylori status or urease activity.  Suppression of H. pylori  might give false negative results . Therefore, the test must no t be used until four weeks without syst emic antibacterial therapy an d two weeks after last dose of acid antisecretory age nts. This is especially important after e radication therapy.
