4.5 Interaction with other medicinal products and other forms of interaction  
 No clinically relevant interactions were observed in clinical trials with desloratadine tablets in which erythromycin or ketoconazole were co -administered (see section  5.1). 
 Paediatric population  Interaction studies have only been performed in adults.  
 In a clinical pharmacology trial , Neoclarityn  tablets  taken concomitantly with alcohol did not potentiate the performance impairing effects of alcohol (see section  5.1). However, cases of alcohol intolerance and intoxication have been reported during post -marketing use. Therefore, caution is recommended if a lcohol is taken concomitantly.
