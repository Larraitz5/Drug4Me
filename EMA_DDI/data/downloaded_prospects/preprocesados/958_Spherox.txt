4.5 Interaction with other medicinal products and other forms of interaction  
 No intera ction studies have been performed.  Locally applied antibiotics or disinfectants may have potential toxicity on articular cartilage and it is not recommended t hat Spherox  comes into direct contact with those substances . In the pivotal studies of Spherox , patients were excluded if they were under medical treatment with corticosteroids.
