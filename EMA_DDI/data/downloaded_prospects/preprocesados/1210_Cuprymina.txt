4.5 Interactions with other medicinal products and other forms of interaction  
 No interactions studies of Copper -64 chloride with other medicinal products have been performed. The possible use of chelating therapies could interfere with the u se of Copper-64- labelled medicinal products. For information concerning interactions associated with the use of Copper-64- labelled medicinal products refer to the Summary of Product Characteristics/package leaflet of the radiolabelled medicinal product.
