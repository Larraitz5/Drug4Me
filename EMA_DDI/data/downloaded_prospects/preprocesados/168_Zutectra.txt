4.5 Interaction with other medicinal products and other forms of interaction  
 Live attenuated virus vaccines  
 Immunoglobulin administration may interfere with the development of an immune response to live attenuated virus vaccines such as rubella, mumps, measles and varicella for a period of 3 months. After administration of this medicinal product, an interval of at least 3 months should elapse before vaccination with live attenuated virus vaccines.  
 Human hepatitis B immunoglobulin should be administrated three to four weeks after vaccination with such a live attenuated vaccine; in case administration of human hepatitis B immunoglobulin is 5 essential within three to four weeks after vaccinat ion, then revaccination should be performed three months after the administration of human hepatitis B immunoglobulin.
